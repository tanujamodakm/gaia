WHAT IS GAIA ?

"Great Awakening Intelligence Atlas" transforms the Great Awakening Map into a searchable network of interconnected ideas using data visualization and analysis.

❝ The truth is hidden in plain sight ❞

The stages of data question the unknown. My interest in solving and recognizing patterns led me to discover this map—which might or might not exist.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the extracted dataset by summarizing nodes, categories, clusters and subcategories. Interactive visualizations enable users to explore how information is distributed, identify dominant themes, and compare structural characteristics through intuitive charts and filters.

Network Analysis

While summary statistics explain what exists within the dataset, network analysis reveals how everything connects. By representing concepts as nodes and their relationships as edges, the dashboard uncovers influential topics, community structures, and information pathways that would otherwise remain hidden. This perspective transforms the original map into an interpretable knowledge network.
