About GAIA

GAIA was developed to organize and analyse the Great Awakening Map by transforming its complex visual information into structured data. The project combines data extraction, classification, dashboard creation, and network analysis to reveal hidden patterns and meaningful relationships between concepts.

Executive Dashboard

Power BI Dashboard Overview
The Executive Dashboard provides an overview of the extracted dataset through KPI cards, category distributions, treemaps, summary tables, and interactive visual analytics. It enables users to understand the overall structure and composition of the Great Awakening Intelligence Atlas at a glance.

Network Analysis

Knowledge Graph & Relationship Analysis
Network Analysis uncovers how concepts are interconnected throughout the dataset. By visualizing nodes, links, and communities, the dashboard highlights influential entities, clusters, and hidden relationships that are difficult to identify from the original map alone.
